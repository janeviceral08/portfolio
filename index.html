Property Management System

Property Management System is a comprehensive hotel solution with dedicated apps for different roles. The admin app manages room availability, pricing, discounts, menus, and reports. The front desk app tracks sales summaries, incoming guests, walk-in bookings, guest stay details (number of days, food availed, extra charges), and room availability by type. All guest purchases are recorded by the front desk so that a complete stay summary is available at checkout. The housekeeping app notifies staff when rooms are ready for cleaning and provides a checklist to ensure quality before marking a room as clean. Together, these apps streamline hotel operations, improve guest experience, and enhance efficiency across departments.
